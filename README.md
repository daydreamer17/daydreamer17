I am a final-year undergraduate at **Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT)** majoring in **Internet of Things Engineering**, and an incoming **Master’s student** at the **National University of Singapore (NUS)** in the **AI Systems** program.

My background began with **IoT and systems engineering**, but over time my interests gradually shifted toward **LLMs, agent systems, and reinforcement learning**. Recently, I have been focusing more on **Agentic RL** — especially how language models can learn to reason, use tools, interact with environments, and improve decision-making over multi-step trajectories.

I am particularly interested in the intersection of **LLM capability, agent systems, and reinforcement learning**: how to make models not only generate fluent responses, but also **plan better, act better, and learn better** in complex workflows. I enjoy studying topics such as **reasoning**, **tool use**, **retrieval**, **memory**, **credit assignment**, and **post-training for agents and LLMs**.
